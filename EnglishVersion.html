Presentation

I'm a visiting assistant professor at University of California Merced, I'm working with Arnold Kim and Shilpa Khatri in the Applied Maths Department on the modeling and simulation of the electromagnetic near field in plasmonic structures. I did my PhD in applied mathematics under the direction of Anne-Sophie Bonnet BenDhia and Patrick Ciarlet, at team POems (ENSTA ParisTech, France). I also worked with Lucas Chesnel as a post-doc in team DEFI (CMAP, France).
